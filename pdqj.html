PDQj

PDQj is based on the retired Gamewright game called PDQ.

This version shuffles the deck and flips the cards for you!

Instructions

Basic Instructions

PDQj is a word-based card game. For each round, 3 letter-cards are flipped simultaneously.

The object of the game is to make a word using the letters in order. That order may be forwards or backwards.

The first letter of the word must start with one of the outside letters.

For example, drawing the letters T-H-S, you might make the word "these" or "shoot", among many others.

The first person to call out a valid word wins that round.

If two people call out valid words simultaneously, the longer word wins.

If no player can make a word, or no winner can be decided, press "No Answer" and the cards will be reshuffled into the deck.

Each 'X' card features an extra letter 'E' that you may use to begin a word.

To get a feel for the game, click through a Practice round (selected by default).

When you are ready to challenge your opponents, click "Keep Score".

This game allows up to four players to compete. There are 26 rounds.

Recommendations

Usually proper nouns are not acceptable.

Try playing with a minimum word length of five letters.

One guess per-person per-round can prevent confusion.

These are just suggestions. Alter gameplay in any way you choose.
